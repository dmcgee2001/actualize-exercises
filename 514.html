Chili Recipe

Search for grocery stores near you!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Beans

Directions

Set the stove to medium-high temperature. Throw all components in pot and put pot on stove. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and let simmer for two hours, stirring occasionally.

Recommended Side Dish

Home Made Beef Yogurt is an excellent pairing to this soup. All you will need is:

  1. Non-Fat Greek Yogurt
  2. 1lb Raw Beef
  3. Blueberries
Combine all of these in a bowl and mix and you have the most delicious blueberry, tar-tar yogurt!
